Our Supercargo / Port Captain service is an economical service that “puts you there” providing a crucial link between the vessels cleaning operation and your vessel operator. Our Port Captain also experience for services the Bulk Cargo (Nickel Ore, Coal, Iron Ore, Bauxite).
